Skulduggery Pleasant

Overview

Skulduggery Pleasant is a captivating book series by Derek Landy. It excels in world-building by introducing a modern-day Earth inhabited by magic, secret societies, and a witty, skeleton detective. The series' blend of humor, magic, and memorable characters immerses readers in a world where the supernatural is ordinary, making it a standout in the urban fantasy genre.


Mystery through Magic

Skulduggery Pleasant is a world where magic is hidden from the common person, throughout the series you will find magic hidden in places you would never expect. This is made possible by not creating too many rules around the world and magic allowing anything to be possible.
