Mistborn

Overview

Mistborn is an epic fantasy book series by Brandon Sanderson, celebrated for its world-building. It unfolds in a world with a unique magic system based on metals, where a dark lord rules. The series' rich lore, intricate characters, and exploration of the magic system and history of the world create a captivating and immersive fantasy world.


History of Another World

Specifically in the first trilogy, history within the world has been lost and the main cast are tasked with gathering knowledge of the past to understand the world and its magic. This creates an immersive experience where the reader can learn about the world along side the characters in the story.
