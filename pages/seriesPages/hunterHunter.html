Hunter x Hunter

Overview

Hunter x Hunter is a renowned anime and manga series known for its intricate world-building. It introduces a diverse array of lands, unique cultures, and a complex examination of the Hunter Association's role in this world. The series' focus on character development, Nen abilities, exploring, scheming and problem solving.


Systems of the Supernatural

While most of Hunter x Hunter's world building is creative but simple, one aspect it excels in is the rules and systems of its world. The story introduces the Nen power system, then uses it to create another power system with the Greed Island arc and even creating a fictional board game similar to chess.
