Overwatch

Overview

Overwatch is a first-person shooter game, it demonstrates world-building by presenting a futuristic Earth where heroes with unique abilities battle to protect the world. The game's rich lore, engaging characters, and vibrant maps create an immersive gaming universe, contributing to its appeal as a character-driven multiplayer experience.


World Building through Character Stories

As the world of Overwatch is set in a futuristic version of our world, there is history we aren't aware of. Overwatch focuses on individual characters and the events that surround them, as we meet characters from all around the world, we build an understanding of the world of Overwatch.
